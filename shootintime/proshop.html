MASTER GUNSMITH: PHILIP SCHMOOTZ

Philip "Schmootzie" Schmootz is our local gunsmith extraordinare. He can repair just about anything. He is master craftsman when it comes to fabricating parts. He is famous for his ability to make precision wares such as hammers, springs, pins, and screws. Schmootzie is especially skilled in recreating accurate fires arms from the mid to late nineteenth century.

When it comes to helping you purchase your first weapon or a new, a used or collectors weapon, Schmootzie and his staff are the best at asking the right questions to help you find the perfect purchase at a reasonable price.
