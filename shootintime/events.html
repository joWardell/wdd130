Courses & Events

All of our instructors are either NRA or USCCA certified therefore we offer a range of courses from both organizations. We offer instruction in pistols, riffles, shotguns, self-defense and safety. Best of all we have courses specifically designed for women or for men in addition to co-educational classes.

Shootin' Time actively participates in sponsoring and participating in national competitive shooting competitions. Our goal is to promote and prepare competitive individuals to win.
