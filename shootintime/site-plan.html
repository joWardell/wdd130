Overview

Purpose

As a woman, I appreciate the luxury of a whole packaged shooting experience, everything from having a first class pro shop to a variety of course offerings, and, especially, an on site gunsmith.

A professionally designed website to communicate your safe, controlled environment, especially targeting women, will definitely enhance your range attendance. Remember, we are the fastest-growing demographic in the gun-ownership population.

By the way, how do you currently keep track of registrations for events and courses? How helpful will it be to have an online registration process to track clients, events, and shooting lane reservations?

As a Web Designer, I will help your business communicate and attract customers by helping them feel the vibe of your Indoor Shooting Range and encourage them to stop in or book online. After all, we all want to conveniently hone our marksmanship skills without having to four-wheel out to the desert.

Audience

Shootin’ Time Indoor Range offers a local, air conditioned alternative to the thirty minute jaunt
to the closest open range. This is a fabulous opportunity to encourage increased active participation
for women and men who do not have the time or the vehicles to rough out the terrain to keep up with
weapons training.

Mothers, women, especially working women, will greatly benefit from Shootin’ Time Indoor Range as it is
within 15 minutes travel time from most locations in the city. We will plan specific Ladies Classes along
with Couples Nights to encourage team participation and a family bonding experiences.

Home On The Range

Welcome to the only indoor shooting range in Cochise County!

  1. We have 24 Range Lanes and we offer gun rentals.
  2. The Proshop is one of the best in the area!
  3. Stop in and meet our Gunsmith, extraordinare, Philip Schmootz.
  4. Be sure to puruse our Events and Course Calendar!

SAFETY FIRST - RULES, RULES AND MORE RULES!

i.General Safety Rules

  1. 1.Anyone under age 18 unaccompanied by parent (or legal guardian) must have a completed parent authorization form. Each member of your party must be age 6 or over. Minors not accompanied by their parent or legally appointed guardian must have a Child Waiver Form completed in full before arrival and brought with them on the day they visit.
  2. 2.Pregnant women are not permitted on the range.
  3. 3.All firearms are considered loaded at all times.
  4. 4.Always point firearms in a safe direction (downrange, the ground, etc.), until on target and ready to fire.
  5. 5.Always keep your trigger finger straight along the frame until on target and ready to fire.
  6. 6.Always know your target and what is behind it.
  7. 7.Always maintain control of your firearm.
  8. 8.No cell phone use while on the range at anytime for any reason.
  9. 9.NO PHOTOS OR VIDEO TAPPING IS ALLOWED AT ANYTIME IN THE LOBBY OR ON THE RANGE.

Dress Code: FOR SAFETY

  1. NO tank tops,
  2. NO low cut V neck shirts,
  3. NO sleeveless shirts,
  4. NO shorts above the knee,
  5. NO opened toed shoes.

Range Rules

  1. 1.The range officer is in charge of the range at all times.
  2. 2.BE MUZZLE CONSCIOUS at all times. Keep the firearm pointed down range even during the loading/unloading process.
  3. 3.Firearms must be unloaded and cased unless on the firing line. No exceptions.
  4. 4.DO NOT remove the firearm from the booth until it is secured in a holster or gun bag before you leave the range.
  5. 5.DO NOT step out of the booth with an unsecured weapon in your hand.
  6. 6.DO NOT turn and face the lobby with a firearm in your hand.
  7. 7.DO NOT pass guns from booth to booth.
  8. 8.Only ONE person may shoot in a booth at a time. Anyone not shooting must stand behind the person shooting.
  9. 9.DO NOT cross the firing line at any time. Shoot only at the target in your lane. Do not point guns up, down, left, or right.
  10. 10.NO shotguns or black powder firearms.
  11. 11.NO Life like targets or Photos of humans are allowed. Check with range master to see if your targets are allowed.
  12. 12.NO steel core ammunition allowed such as: Wolf, Tula.
  13. 13.NO BB guns, pellet guns or air soft guns.
  14. 14.Cleaning of firearms or gunsmithing in on the range or in the facility is not allowed.
  15. 15.When shooting, keep the muzzle below the red line on the walls.
  16. 16.Do not shoot the floor of the range.
  17. 17.All rounds must impact the black rubber back stop.
  18. 18.Drawing from the holster is prohibited.
  19. 19.No rapid fire. You must control the firearm.
  20. 20.Ear and eye protection must be worn at all times.
  21. 21.Food and drink are prohibited from the range.
  22. 22.Smoking/smokeless tobacco is prohibited in the store and the range.
  23. 23.Sweep your shell casings past the firing line.
  24. 24.You may pick up your own brass.

Events and Courses

All of our instructors are either NRA or USCCA certified therefore we offer a range of courses from both organizations. We offer instruction in pistols, riffles, shotguns, self-defense and safety. Best of all we have courses specifically designed for women, for men in addition to co-educational classes.

Shootin’ Time actively participates in sponsoring and participating in national competitive shooting competitions. Our goal is to promote and prepare competitive individuals to win.

Pro-Shop and On Site Gunsmith

We are a Federal Firearms Licensed business.

Philip Schmootz is our local gunsmith extraordinare. He can repair just about anything. He is master craftsman when it comes to fabricating parts. He is famous for his ability to make precision wares such as hammers, springs, pins, and screws. Schmootzie’s especially skilled in recreating accurate fires arms from the 1800’s.
