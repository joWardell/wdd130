Welcome to our indoor, air conditioned facility. We hope you are ready to experience our commitment to keeping all of our clients safe in our facility and especially on the range. After registering, you will receive a Range Safety Orientation & Tour. It is all part of our high standard of excellence to provide you the best and safest experience.

  1. Submit your Release/Waiver
  2. Complete the Safety Orientation
  3. Join the Safe Shootin' Time Community

Dress Code: FOR SAFETY

  • NO tank tops.
  • NO low cut V neck shirts.
  • NO sleeveless shirts.
  • NO shorts above the knee.
  • NO open toed shoes.

GENERAL SAFETY RULES:

  1. Anyone under age 18 unaccompanied by parent (or legal guardian) must have a completed parent authorization form. Each member of your party must be age 6 or over. Minors not accompanied by their parent or legally appointed guardian must have a Child Waiver Form completed in full before arrival and brought with them on the day they visit.
  2. Pregnant women are not permitted on the range.
  3. All firearms are considered loaded at all times.
  4. Always point firearms in a safe direction (downrange, the ground, etc.), until on target and ready to fire.
  5. Always keep your trigger finger straight along the frame until on target and ready to fire.
  6. Always know your target and what is behind it.
  7. Always maintain control of your firearm.
  8. NO CELL PHONE use while on the range at anytime for any reason.
  9. NO PHOTOS OR VIDEO TAPPING IS ALLOWED AT ANYTIME IN THE LOBBY OR ON THE RANGE.

RANGE RULES

  1. The range officer is in charge of the range at all times.
  2. BE MUZZLE CONSCIOUS at all times. Keep the firearm pointed down range even during the loading/unloading process.
  3. Firearms must be unloaded and cased unless on the firing line. No exceptions.
  4. DO NOT remove the firearm from the booth until it is secured in a holster or gun bag before you leave the range.
  5. DO NOT step out of the booth with an unsecured weapon in your hand.
  6. DO NOT turn and face the lobby with a firearm in your hand.
  7. DO NOT pass guns from booth to booth.
  8. Only ONE person may shoot in a booth at a time. Anyone not shooting must stand behind the person shooting.
  9. DO NOT cross the firing line at any time. Shoot only at the target in your lane. Do not point guns up, down, left, or right.
  10. NO shotguns or black powder firearms.
  11. NO Life like targets or Photos of humans are allowed. Check with range master to see if your targets are allowed.
  12. NO steel core ammunition allowed such as: Wolf, Tula.
  13. NO BB guns, pellet guns or air soft guns.
  14. Cleaning of firearms or gunsmithing in on the range or in the facility is not allowed.
  15. When shooting, keep the muzzle below the red line on the walls.
  16. Do not shoot the floor of the range.
  17. All rounds must impact the black rubber back stop.
  18. Drawing from the holster is prohibited.
  19. No rapid fire. You must control the firearm.
  20. Ear and eye protection must be worn at all times.
  21. Food and drink are prohibited from the range.
  22. Smoking/smokeless tobacco is prohibited in the store and the range.
  23. Sweep your shell casings past the firing line.
  24. You may pick up your own brass.
