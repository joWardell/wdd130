All About Me

Jody

Wanting a couple of certificates I realized that with some effort I could get a second bachelor of science degree in Professional Studies. I am an older, non-traditional student therefore taking my time to pursue additional interests.

Last year, my husband retired from the USAF after 36 years on active duty. He now works for Jacobs and we live in Sierra Vista, Arizona. We have a black lab who 12 years old (Janie Belle) and a chihua-chow who is 4 years old (Jasper), and recently rescued another chihuahua mix who is 18 months old (Juliet Buttercup) from Palominas.

A fun, local discovery we made last year is the Double G Alpaca Ranch. Leroy German works for Jacobs and we met him and his wife, Paula, at a company picnic. They have prize winning alpaca fiber and they offer a fabulous HipCamp site: Camping? Alpaca My Bags.

One of my favorite scriptures is Nephi 11:32,35-36.
