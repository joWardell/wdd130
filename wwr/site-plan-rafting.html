Overview

Purpose

This program is structured to teach the basics of rafting in a safe, encouraging environment for children ages 5-10 years old. We go over terminology, rules, safety, and equipment. We participate in fun activities that encourage skills, boost confidence, and being active in working as part of a team.

Audience

This program is perfect for Parents, married or single, Grandparents, or or Guardians looking to give their child(ren) a fabulous summer time adventure. It is especially great for children who need to gain confidence in expanding their inner resources while building health habits and learning to focus.
