KAYAK ART CAMP

Kayak Art Camp for Tykes is all about using the five senses to blend together physical activity with artistic creativity for a unique experience. It is designed to enhance imaginations, increase physical skills, and allow children to connect and communicate with others.

The week long camp runs from 9 a.m. until 4 p.m. Monday through Friday.Tykes will hit the water with instructors from 9 a.m until Noon. From Noon until 2:30 p.m. the Tykes will enjoy a lunch break and a movie. The last portion of the day will be filled with a variety of art projects, individually and in team projects. Tykes will be introduced to several different art mediums.

THE STAFF

Sally Manders is a local artist and avid kayak enthusiast. Associated with the St. Bernard Cultural Alliance for the last 25 years she continues to inspire students to explore their creative potential.

Instructors are professionals and experienced in the water and trained in skilled kayaking.

Instructors and Staff are First Aid/ CPR certified. Staff ratio is 2 to 6.
